works / about

I'm a multidisciplinary designer with 2+ years of experience in product design. Currently working at Appulate, a US InsureTech product delivering the web platform brings all the insurance players in one place. My goal is to help businesses reach product goals in balance with users' interests.

Prior to product design, I worked as a graphic designer for 5 years (and still help some of my fav clients with design).

For better understanding users and team, I always try to expand my view perspective by learning and using adjacent disciplines: front-end stack, SQL, python, analytics tools, psychology, and sociology.
