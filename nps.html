Net promoter score

Feb-Apr 2020

We launched NPS in 2020 at the first time. It was the first metric in the product that helped the team to know users' loyalty. We relaunched NPS in 2021, the score has increased from -5 to 3.

Ideation & management ‚ú∑ Survey ‚ú∑ Insights analysis

Problem

The product did not have any metrics or researches that can help understand how users evaluate the product. The team was confused not knowing our users. I suggested using NPS. It is a simple metric that can be easily tracked over time. Coupled with the open-ended question "What is the main reason for your score?" it can help identify weaknesses in a product.

First result

We started measuring NPS in february 2020 and collected 602 responses those year. Our score was -5.

Answer analysis

Also we received 172 answers on our question ‚ÄúWhat is the main reason for your score‚Äù. Analysis of these answers was the most important part of this survey because here we had found growth points. We defined common themes in the answers and highlighted categories for the most frequent mentions.

We counted results and defined the most volnurable parts of the product.

A lot of answers was dedicated to Questionnaire functionality. So we highlighted detailed themes for this section.

Outcomes / 2020

We prepared a report for stakeholders and suggested some solutions based on answers. Some of them we had implemented and saw changes in NPS in 2021.

Results in 2021

Outcomes
