Net Promoter score

Feb-Apr 2020

Net Promoter score measures the customers' loyalty with a score from -100 to +100, which comes from answering the question “How likely are you to recommend us to a colleague?”.
We launched a survey, reviewed references, and calculated the final score. We used the findings for correcting product plans.

  • – Survey
  • – Insights analysis

Problem

The product did not have any metrics or researches that can help understand how users evaluate the product. The team was confused not knowing our users. I suggested using NPS. It is a simple metric that can be easily tracked over time. Coupled with the open-ended question "What is the main reason for your score?" it can help identify weaknesses in a product.

Process

I presented an idea to stakeholders and launched the survey for one month to faster taking results. We received 602 total responses.

Process

NPS was -5. For insurance industry benchmark is between 19 and 68. Our score was not good but was not the end of the world. We defined a started point where we can start measure and change user loyalty.

Analysis

We received 172 answers to our question “What is the main reason for your score”. Analysis of these answers is the most important part of this survey because here we can find growth points.
We defined common themes in the answers and highlighted categories for the most frequent mentions.

Analysis

We counted results and defined the most vulnerable parts of the product. A lot of answers were dedicated to Questionnaire functionality and we highlighted detailed themes for this section. After that, we formed several fast solutions that can help close some problems that we defined. E.g. we launched Help Center that really helps users in working with product. For the bigger problems, we started to dive deeper into research.

Outcomes

NPS helped us answer how users evaluate our product. But more importantly, we found the problem points that affect the score and on which we can work. We are going to restart NPS after the next 6 months and compare scores in dynamic.
